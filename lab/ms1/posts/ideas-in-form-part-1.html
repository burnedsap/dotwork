Ideas in Form Part 1

12 Nov 2020

I haven't yet figured out an idea, but I've found the question I'd like to answer.

What does 'healthy' technology look like?

But crucially, for whom? Technology has been shaped by a few to serve the needs of many. I think there's a lot of value in understanding how it would look like if it was designed for a single person, and their specific needs.

A small slide deck with a few scenarios to explain my line of thinking.
