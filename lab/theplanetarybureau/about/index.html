About

The Planetary Bureau is humanity's bridge into the post-human future. The plantary cycles of change are upon us, and we are to undergo a great upheaval. We are not destined to survive the cycles of distruption, but our legacy will live on. The never ending search for our existence will come to a close as we gain a deeper understanding of the planet and our role in it's eventual future.
