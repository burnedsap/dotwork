hello

I’m Salil Parekh, a versatile designer with many talents. But talk is cheap, and I’d rather you judge for yourself.

I’m a graduate of the National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad, and studied visual communication, spatial design, and industrial design.

I’ve since worked at the Devi Art Gallery, Coca-Cola India, FLYP@MTV, and The Busride, and Quicksand Design Studio.

So far I’ve worked in the fields of visual communication, spatial design, generative art, new media, emerging technologies, machine learning, human centred design, speculative design, and SRH to name a few.

This space is still under construction, and doesn’t work that well, mostly because I’m the one making everything. So uh, do carry on, but beware of broken code and unresolved errors.
