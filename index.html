Hello, my name is Salil Parekh and I'm a versatile designer who is currently looking to explore the intersection between design thinking and technology.

I currently work for Quicksand Design Studios, and have worked with organisations including UNCDF, UNICEF, Lowe's, Honeywell, British Council, Prince Claus Fund, Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, Humanitarian Leadership Academy and Médecins Sans Frontières.

I'm looking for opportunities to explore this intersections between design thinking and technology, potentially contextual design, design through futures thinking, healthy technology amongst others.
